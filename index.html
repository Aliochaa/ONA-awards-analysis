Since 2000, the Online Journalism Awards (OJAs) honor excellence in digital journalism around the world. They're decerned during the Awards banquet at the annual ONA conference.

According to the ONA website, "the OJAs have recognized major media, international and independent sites and individuals producing innovative work in multimedia storytelling. The OJA Committee and judges place special emphasis on entries that demonstrate mastery of the special characteristics and emerging technology of digital journalism."

Rediscover the fantastic works submitted to the judges over the past decade with our ONA Awards Bot. A special thank to the designers and coders Sisi Wei and Jeremy Bowers !

How we got the data

The complete official list of finalists and awarded is available on the ONA website. After a web scraping and a big cleaning task, we got a list of 1230 pieces of work on a spreadsheet. It's available here but be careful, reconciling data could have generated some small errors and finalists's awards titles aren't displayed. We used a Python script to automatically test every link.

The charts only represent media with at least 3 awards won.
